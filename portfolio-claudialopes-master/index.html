About me

I'm a design student, currently on the 2nd year of my bachelor in visual arts and technologies from the higher school of education in Lisbon. I am passionate about design and I love to create and explore all forms of art which influences the results of my work as a designer. During the past 2 years of my education, I’ve learned different ways to create content that people will enjoy, with my biggest goal being to create an impact in people’s life’s and make a difference. I have explored different forms of art, from painting and sculpture to 3D modulation, graphic and product design or digital painting which, together with my passion and creativity, gives me skills to reach design solutions that are functional and visually appealing.
